Grogu: Community K9 Ambassador

Grogu is more than just a working dog-he’s a symbol of partnership and trust between our department and the community. Whether he’s on patrol, helping in searches, or making friends at local events, Grogu brings smiles everywhere he goes!

Fun Facts About Grogu

  • Loves wearing themed bandanas and vests for parades and school visits!
  • Specially trained in search & rescue, detection, and public demonstrations.
  • Always ready to meet kids and seniors at community events.

A Day in the Life: K9 Officer & Grogu vs. Regular Officer

K9 Officer & Grogu Regular Officer
Morning Routine Feeding, walking, bonding, and K9 training Briefing, equipment check
Training K9 drills: obedience, scent detection, agility Standard law enforcement training
Patrol Duties Responds to calls, assists in searches, community events Responds to calls, patrols assigned areas
Community Engagement Public demos, school visits, meet & greets (often in costume!) Community events, but less likely to draw crowds
After Hours Grogu goes home with handler, ongoing care & playtime End of shift, goes home

Why K9 Teams Matter

  • K9 teams help break down barriers-people are more likely to approach and talk to an officer with a friendly dog.
  • Grogu assists in searches, crowd safety, and public education.
  • K9s like Grogu are trained for thousands of hours to ensure safety, reliability, and positive community impact.
